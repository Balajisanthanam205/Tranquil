About Us

Welcome to our groundbreaking rail monitoring platform, an innovation aimed at enhancing railway safety and operational efficiency. Our solution utilizes state-of-the-art AI-based acoustic wave technology to monitor rail tracks in real-time, allowing for the early detection of cracks and other rail defects. This system ensures timely maintenance, preventing catastrophic failures and significantly improving the longevity and reliability of rail infrastructure. By providing accurate, real-time data on track health, we aim to create a safer, smarter railway system that benefits both operators and passengers.


This project is part of our participation in the Smart India Hackathon, where we are dedicated to advancing the future of rail transport through technology. We are passionate about leveraging modern sensor systems, AI, and data analytics to solve real-world challenges. Our platform not only detects defects but also helps predict wear and tear, ensuring that railway operators can take proactive measures to maintain the highest safety standards. Join us as we work towards revolutionizing the way railroads are monitored and maintained.
